CEC Engineer Isaac Clarke

Isaac Clarke looking backwards and in front of him a colony of Unitologists

Isaac Clarke CEC Engineer, the only survivor of USG Ishimura after the Necromorph outbreak caused by the retrieval of an unknown artifact called The Black Marker considered by Unitologists key of human existence.
